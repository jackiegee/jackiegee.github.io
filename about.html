About Us

2 Oceans is a owner operated business with 24 years of experience in the access control industry. We have been accredited Centurion installers since 1996. All equipment and workmanship is guaranteed and installed professionally, to the required industry standards.

We perform installations, maintenance as well as monthly/quarterly service contracts (preventative maintenance) to access control equipment. Free quotations and professional advice on home security requirements. Competitive rates and a 24 month guarantee on select Centurion products.
