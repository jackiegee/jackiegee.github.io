Welcome to Sunset Villas at Conch Shell. Located in the northern region of St. Lucia, Sunset Villas is is situated in the the town of Gros-Islet town village - the heart and soul of beautiful St. Lucia .

Our Story

Sunset Villas was developed by Daphne and Philip Hippster who were both born and raised in Gros-Islet, St. Lucia. The villa was originally known as Daphils and was a three-story, 10 room guest house with a modest Bed and Breakfast.

Over the years, the demand for short term rentals grew tremendously in St. Lucia, particularly in the northern region of the island - as people wanted to be near all the action and night life that St. Lucia had to offer.

After extending the propery and undergoing a two year renovation phase, Daphils was converted to 8 modern one and two bedroom villas and renamed Sunset Villas at Conch Shell.

Since its opening in 2010, Sunset Villas has been the go to spot for visitors from all over the world wanting to experience a slice of St. Lucia. Though the villas are modern, they were built with some local "flava" and visitors return year after year for this luxurious yet home-like feel. Wheter it is families seeking adventure, couples looking for romance and relaxation or friends needing that much needed get-away, Sunset Villas at Conch Shell provides it all.
